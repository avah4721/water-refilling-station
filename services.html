Frequently Asked Questions

What is your water purification process?

We use a 7-stage filtration process including sediment filtration, carbon filtration, reverse osmosis, UV sterilization, and ozonation to ensure the highest quality drinking water.

How long does delivery take?

Orders placed before 2 PM are delivered the same day. Orders after 2 PM will be delivered the next business day. Delivery typically takes 1-2 hours within our service area.

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription anytime. Changes will take effect on your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery/pickup, GCash, Maya, and bank transfers. Online payments are processed securely through our payment portal.
